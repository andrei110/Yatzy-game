Yatzy Game

Student version

  • The goal of this game is to arrive at 1000 points. For that you have to pass 3 thresholds which are at 100 points, 400 points and 700 points. For the first threshold you need to acumulate 100 points. After that, when you arrive between 300 and 400 points, respectively 600 and 700 points you must overtake the threshold to move forward.
  • If you roll the dices and it lands 1 or 5 or you hit 3 of a kind, 4 of a kind or 5 of a kind you can hold your points and let the next player's turn begin or you can roll the remaining dices for additional points with the risk of losing them all.
  • If you overtake a player you receive 50 points and he is deducted 50 points.
  • If the overtaken player has more than 900 points, the previous rule is canceled.
